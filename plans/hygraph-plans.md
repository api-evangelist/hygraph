# Hygraph Plans

Hygraph offers three tiers: a free Hobby plan for individuals and experimenters, a Growth plan for teams, and custom Enterprise pricing for large organizations.

## Hobby (Free)

- **Price:** $0 / month, free forever
- **Content Entries:** 1,000
- **API Calls:** 500,000 / month
- **Seats:** 3
- **Locales:** 2
- **Models:** 20
- **Components:** 10
- **Environments:** 1
- **Webhooks:** 5
- **API Tokens:** 3
- **Asset Storage:** Unlimited
- **Asset Upload Size:** 50 MB per asset
- **Rate Limit (uncached):** 5 requests/sec
- **Roles:** 2 standard roles
- **Features:** Live preview, commenting, assignment workflow
- **Version Retention:** Not included
- **Support:** Community support
- **Overage:** Usage blocked when limits are reached until the next billing period

## Growth ($199 / month)

- **Price:** $199 / month (billed monthly or annually)
- **Content Entries:** 10,000
- **API Calls:** 1,000,000 / month
- **Seats:** 10
- **Locales:** 3
- **Models:** 40
- **Components:** 20
- **Environments:** 2
- **Webhooks:** 10
- **API Tokens:** 5
- **Asset Storage:** Unlimited
- **Asset Upload Size:** 200 MB per asset
- **Rate Limit (uncached):** 25 requests/sec
- **Roles:** 4 standard roles
- **Features:** Remote source connection, 14-day version retention, email support
- **Overage (API Operations):** $0.20 per 1,000,000 additional API calls
- **Overage (Asset Traffic):** $0.20 per GB
- **Support:** Email support

## Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

- **Price:** Custom — contact sales
- **Content Entries:** 1,000,000+
- **API Calls:** 50,000,000+ / month
- **Seats:** Up to 200
- **Locales:** Up to 80
- **Models:** Up to 500
- **Components:** Up to 150
- **Environments:** Up to 10
- **Webhooks:** Up to 100
- **API Tokens:** Up to 30
- **Asset Upload Size:** Configurable
- **Rate Limit (uncached):** Up to 500 requests/sec (higher on dedicated clusters)
- **Features:** SSO, multitenancy, custom workflows, audit logs, 24/7 monitoring, dedicated support, up to 99.95% uptime SLA, dedicated clusters, custom rate limits
- **Version Retention:** Up to 365 days
- **Support:** Dedicated support, 24/7 monitoring

## Payment Options

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.)
- Bank transfer available for Enterprise
- Currency: USD standard; EUR available for European customers
- No credit card required for Hobby signup
